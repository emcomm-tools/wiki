Q-Codes Reference

Q-codes are three-letter codes starting with "Q" used as shorthand in amateur radio communication. They can be statements or questions depending on context.

Usage Tips

  • QRL? - Always ask before transmitting on a frequency
  • QRZ? - Ask "who is calling?" when you miss a callsign
  • QSY - Suggest moving to another frequency
  • QRT - Indicate you're signing off
  • 73 - Not a Q-code, but means "Best regards" (end of contact)
