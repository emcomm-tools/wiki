Learning Tips

  • Learn by sound - Don't count dits and dahs
  • Start at target speed - Use Farnsworth spacing
  • Practice daily - 15-30 minutes consistent beats marathon sessions
  • Copy on paper - Then verify
  • Listen to real QSOs - WebSDRs, on-air practice
  • Send what you copy - Reinforces learning
Koch Method: Learn two characters at full speed, add one character when you reach 90% accuracy. Don't memorize lookup tables!
