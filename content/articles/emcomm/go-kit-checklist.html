Packing Tips

üì¶ Kit Organization

  • Use clear containers - See contents without opening
  • Label everything - Especially cables and connectors
  • Modular packing - Radio kit, antenna kit, power kit, etc.
  • Grab-and-go bags - Pre-packed for different scenarios
  • Inventory list - Laminated, inside each container

‚ö° Power Strategy

  • Calculate needs - See Off-Grid Power article
  • Keep batteries charged - Monthly maintenance
  • Test solar before you go - Don't discover problems in the field
  • Multiple charging sources - AC, 12V vehicle, solar

  • ‚òê All batteries charged?
  • ‚òê Radios programmed with local frequencies?
  • ‚òê Antennas tested?
  • ‚òê Software/apps up to date?
  • ‚òê Personal medications packed?
  • ‚òê Weather forecast checked?
  • ‚òê Family notified of deployment?
  • ‚òê Fuel in vehicle?
Test Your Kit! Don't wait for an emergency to discover problems. Set up your complete kit at least twice a year. Practice deployment. Time yourself. Fix issues before they matter.
Post-Event: After every deployment, restock consumables, recharge batteries, and note any equipment issues. Update your kit while lessons are fresh!
