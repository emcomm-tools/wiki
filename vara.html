VARA Modem

VARA is a high-performance sound card modem developed by José Alberto Nieto Ros (EA5HVK). It's the fastest amateur radio HF modem available, widely used with Winlink and VarAC.

Versions

  • VARA HF - For HF bands (3-30 MHz), up to 8,490 bps
  • VARA FM - For VHF/UHF FM, up to 25,000 bps

Installation in EmComm-Tools

  1. Download from https://rosmodem.wordpress.com/
  2. Run: wine VARA_setup.exe
  3. Configure audio device (Digirig, Signalink)
  4. Set PTT method (CAT, VOX, or COM port)

Audio Settings

  • TX Drive: ALC just begins to move
  • RX Level: Green zone in VARA meter
  • Start at 50%, adjust as needed
